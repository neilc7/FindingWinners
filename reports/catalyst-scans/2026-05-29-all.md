## Executive Summary

This is the first real catalyst scan. Five key findings:

1. **IREN is the biggest mismatch on the watchlist.** The company signed $13.1B in contracted AI cloud revenue ($9.7B Microsoft + $3.4B NVIDIA) and a 5GW strategic NVIDIA partnership, but the stock *fell* 6.77% after earnings because current quarterly revenue is only $144.8M (still mostly Bitcoin mining). This looks like the DELL Feb-2026 pattern: massive contracted pipeline, small current numbers, market skeptical. If they execute on 480MW deployment by year-end, the re-rating could be dramatic.

2. **Bloom Energy (BE) is becoming the standard for AI data center power.** Revenue doubled (+130% YoY), Oracle chose Bloom exclusively for the multi-gigawatt Project Jupiter campus, Nebius signed a $2.6B deal, and Brookfield committed $5B. The "bring your own power" trend is accelerating faster than expected, and Bloom is the primary beneficiary.

3. **Salesforce (CRM) Agentforce crossed $1.2B ARR (+205% YoY) but the stock barely moved.** Revenue slightly missed, Tableau and Commerce Cloud were soft, and the stock dipped ~1% after-hours. The market is fixated on legacy business softness and ignoring the AI transformation happening underneath. Classic muted-reaction pattern.

4. **Rubrik (RBRK) reports earnings June 4 — positioning at the intersection of data security and AI agent governance is underappreciated.** The new Agent Cloud product governs agentic AI deployments. This is a new security surface that barely existed 6 months ago. The market still views Rubrik as a backup company.

5. **Dell (DELL) confirmed the AI server thesis beyond any doubt** with 757% AI server revenue growth and a raised $60B FY27 target. Stock surged 38% after-hours on May 28. The original thesis from the Feb-26 earnings played out exactly as described in `goal.md`. The question now is whether there's another leg up or if it's priced in.

### IREN — IREN Limited
- **Score: 21/25** (Info: 5 | Quality: 3 | Valuation: 3 | Catalyst: 5 | Sentiment: 5)
- **Signal:** Major Positive
- **Mismatch:** YES — Massive contracted revenue pipeline, stock fell after earnings. Market focused on current small numbers and Bitcoin-to-AI transition risk.

#### What's New
IREN reported Q3 FY26 (quarter ended March 31, 2026) on May 7:
- Total revenue of $144.8M (down from $184.7M QoQ as Bitcoin mining revenue declined)
- AI Cloud Services revenue of $33.6M (up from $17.3M QoQ — nearly doubled)
- Net loss of $247.8M

But the headline numbers don't tell the story. The catalysts are:
- **$3.4B 5-year AI Cloud contract with NVIDIA** — deploying air-cooled Blackwell GPUs at Childress, TX. Targeting ramp from early 2027.
- **5GW strategic partnership with NVIDIA** — collaboration to deploy NVIDIA-aligned infrastructure across IREN's global pipeline. NVIDIA received a 5-year option to invest up to $2.1B at $70/share.
- **$9.7B 5-year AI Cloud contract with Microsoft** signed last fall
- **$3.1B ARR already under contract**, targeting $3.7B by end of CY2026
- **$1.6B Dell GPU purchase** for Blackwell systems at Childress, commissioning early 2027
- Upon Blackwell commissioning, ARR target rises from $3.7B to $4.4B
- Acquired Mirantis to strengthen AI cloud deployment/management
- Acquired Nostrum adding 490MW in Spain and GW+ development pipeline

Stock declined 6.77% on earnings day despite these announcements, and fell further in after-hours to ~$59.50.

#### Variant Perception
**Street thinks:** Execution risk. Revenue is tiny ($145M/quarter) vs. contracted pipeline ($13B+). Bitcoin mining still majority of revenue. Capital-intensive transition with large losses. Prove it first.

**What might actually be happening:** IREN has quietly assembled one of the most valuable data center pipelines in the market. 5GW of secured power, contracts with the two biggest AI spenders (Microsoft and NVIDIA), and NVIDIA willing to invest $2.1B at $70/share is an enormous validation. The Bitcoin-to-AI pivot is happening fast — AI cloud revenue nearly doubled QoQ. By year-end 2026, they'll have 480MW operational and 150,000 GPUs deployed. The current stock price doesn't remotely reflect $3.7-4.4B in contracted ARR.

**Underappreciated positive:** NVIDIA's option to invest $2.1B at $70/share is a powerful signal. NVIDIA doesn't invest in companies it expects to fail. This also guarantees IREN supply chain priority for future GPU platforms.

#### Upcoming Catalysts
- **1 month:** Horizon 1-4 data center construction updates. Q4 FY26 earnings (~Aug 2026)
- **3 months:** 480MW capacity target checkpoint. Early Blackwell GPU deployments
- **6 months:** Air-cooled Blackwell commissioning at Childress (early 2027). ARR approaching $4.4B
- **12 months:** 1,210MW expansion underway (Childress Horizons 5-6, Sweetwater 1). 2028+ 5GW global pipeline

#### Bull Case
IREN has $13.1B in contracted AI cloud revenue with two of the most creditworthy counterparties on Earth (Microsoft and NVIDIA). If they execute on 480MW by year-end and Blackwell commissioning in early 2027, the stock at ~$60 with $4.4B ARR run-rate would be absurdly cheap compared to peers like NBIS. NVIDIA's willingness to invest $2.1B at $70/share provides a near-term floor. This could be a 3-5x from here over 18 months.

#### Bear Case
The transition from Bitcoin mining to AI cloud is capital-intensive and fraught with execution risk. Current net loss of $247.8M per quarter is alarming. Data center construction timelines could slip. The company needs to prove it can actually deploy and operate GPU clusters at scale, not just sign contracts. If execution falters, the stock could easily revisit $30-40.

#### Recommendation
- **Action:** Keep watching closely — this is the highest-conviction mismatch on the watchlist. Consider building a position on dips.
- **Follow-up:** Monitor Horizon 1-4 delivery progress. Watch for Q4 FY26 earnings (~Aug) for AI cloud revenue acceleration. Key milestone: does AI cloud revenue cross $100M/quarter by Q4?

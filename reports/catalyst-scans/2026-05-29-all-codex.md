# Catalyst Scan — 2026-05-29 — Codex Independent

## Executive Summary

This pass was done independently against `watchlist.md` using recent web research. My top conclusion is similar to the prior scan: the best opportunities are not the biggest AI winners, but the names where new business evidence is ahead of market trust.

Top findings:

1. **IREN remains the clearest mismatch.** The Microsoft and NVIDIA AI-cloud contracts, NVIDIA warrant structure, Dell GPU purchase, and 5 GW power pipeline are real catalysts. The risk is execution and financing, not demand.
2. **Bloom Energy is a real AI power beneficiary.** Oracle, Brookfield, and Nebius validate behind-the-meter power as an AI bottleneck trade. I rank it highly, but less as a hidden mismatch and more as a confirmed thematic winner.
3. **CRM has a plausible AI-underappreciation setup.** Agentforce crossing $1B ARR and AI/Data ARR growth matter. The market may still be treating Salesforce like an ex-growth SaaS incumbent.
4. **RBRK and NOW are strong “AI governance/control plane” candidates.** Both have credible enterprise AI narratives, but I want more customer adoption metrics before treating either as a high-conviction mismatch.
5. **DELL, DOCN, NBIS, ALAB, VRT, RKLB, and NVDA are mostly confirmed stories.** The businesses look strong, but the framework is hunting for early catalysts before the move; most of these have already re-rated.

## Detailed Scans

### IREN — IREN Limited
- **Score:** 21/25 (Info 5 | Quality 3 | Valuation 3 | Catalyst 5 | Sentiment 5)
- **Signal:** Major Positive
- **Mismatch:** Yes

#### What's New
IREN announced a $9.7B Microsoft AI-cloud contract in November 2025, a $3.4B NVIDIA AI-cloud contract in May 2026, a strategic NVIDIA collaboration across a 5 GW power portfolio, warrants for NVIDIA tied to GPU deployment milestones, and a $1.6B Dell purchase for Blackwell systems. The Q3 FY26 release also showed $144.8M revenue and a large GAAP loss, which explains why investors remain skeptical.

Sources: [IREN Q3 FY26 results](https://investors.iren.com/news-releases/news-release-details/iren-reports-financial-results-third-quarter-fy26), [IREN NVIDIA contract](https://investors.iren.com/news-releases/news-release-details/iren-signs-34-billion-ai-cloud-services-contract-nvidia), [IREN Microsoft contract](https://investors.iren.com/news-releases/news-release-details/iren-signs-97-billion-ai-cloud-services-contract-microsoft), [IREN 5 GW NVIDIA partnership](https://investors.iren.com/news-releases/news-release-details/iren-announces-nvidia-strategic-partnership-accelerate-ai), [Dell GPU purchase](https://investors.iren.com/news-releases/news-release-details/iren-signs-16-billion-gpu-purchase-agreement-dell-technologies)

#### Variant Perception
The market is probably right to demand proof: converting contracted revenue into operating AI-cloud revenue is hard. But the customer quality and NVIDIA alignment are unusually strong. This is the best “old narrative vs. new fundamentals” setup on the list.

#### Catalysts
- **1 month:** Construction and deployment updates for Childress/Horizon data centers.
- **3 months:** AI cloud revenue trajectory and Q4 FY26 results.
- **6 months:** Evidence that GPU deployment milestones are being hit.
- **12 months:** Blackwell commissioning and ARR conversion.

#### Recommendation
Keep at the top of the research queue. Do not treat NVIDIA warrants as a price floor; treat them as validation contingent on execution.
